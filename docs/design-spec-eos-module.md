## Overview

The EOS Module is The Brain's dedicated section for running the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS) as described in *Traction* by Gino Wickman. MHMW begins EOS training the week of July 28, 2026 with Implementor **Margaret Dixon** (Boulder, CO).

The module is built as a **container first** — the structure is in place and ready to receive data as the team works through the EOS process with Margaret. Nothing is locked in yet. The Accountability Chart and V/TO will be populated after the first meeting.

**Carmen Miranda is the EOS assistant.** She monitors Rocks, flags at-risk items, preps L10 meeting agendas, and helps the Integrator stay on top of the system between meetings.

## Module Structure — 6 Tabs

---

### Tab 1: Rocks

**Purpose:** Track all company and individual Rocks for the current quarter.

**Quarter display:** Q3 2026 (Jul 1 – Sep 30) · Days remaining counter

**Company Rocks section:**
- Rock name
- Owner (assigned leadership team member)
- Due date
- 4-step progress bar: Defined → In Progress → Review → Complete
- Status badge: On Track (green) / At Risk (yellow) / Off Track (red)

**Individual Rocks section:**
- Grouped by person
- Same progress bar and status badge
- Carmen flag if a Rock moves to At Risk or Off Track

**Rock Stars section:**
- Employees who completed their Rocks on time in the previous quarter
- Photo, name, Rock name, completion date
- Posted to Company News Feed when a Rock is completed

---

### Tab 3: Issues List (IDS)

**Purpose:** The Issues List is where problems, obstacles, and opportunities are captured so they can be Identified, Discussed, and Solved (IDS) in the L10 meeting.

**Columns:** Issue · Owner · Priority · Date Added · Status

**Priority levels:** Critical · High · Medium · Low

**Status:** Open · In IDS · Resolved

**Carmen Integration:** Carmen can add issues automatically when she detects patterns — e.g., if 3+ submittals go overdue in a week, she adds "Submittal tracking process" to the Issues List.

### Tab 6: Rock Stars

**Purpose:** Recognition wall for employees who are crushing their Rocks and living the company's Core Values.

**Display:**

- Name, role, Rock or achievement

## EOS Widget — Employee Home Page

A condensed version of the Rocks tab appears as a card on every employee's home page:

- Carmen Miranda 🍌 banner with Q3 countdown
- Each of the employee's Rocks with 4-step progress bar and status badge
- Footer: "N/N Rocks On Track · X days to L10"
- "Go to EOS" link that opens the full EOS Module

---

## L10 Meeting Support

Carmen Miranda assists with L10 meeting prep:

1. **48 hours before L10:** Carmen sends a reminder to all leadership team members to update their Rock statuses
2. **24 hours before L10:** Carmen generates a meeting prep summary — Scorecard status, Rock statuses, Issues List sorted by priority
3. **During L10:** The Issues List is the working document — items can be marked "In IDS" and "Resolved" in real time
4. **After L10:** Carmen logs the meeting summary and any new Rocks or Issues added
